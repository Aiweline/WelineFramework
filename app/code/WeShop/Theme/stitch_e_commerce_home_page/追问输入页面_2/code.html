Sony Alpha a7 III Full Frame Mirrorless Camera

Original Question

Does this camera support 4k recording without overheating?
Asked by PhotographyFan99 • Sep 21, 2023

Top Answer

Yes, it supports 4k at 30fps. I've recorded for about 45 minutes straight in a climate-controlled room (72'F) without any overheating warnings. However, if you are shooting in direct sunlight or 4K 60fps (which this model doesn't do natively uncropped), your mileage may vary. High-speed SD cards are a must.
