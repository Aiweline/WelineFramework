Our Journey

Founded in 2010 in a small garage in Seattle, Amazonia began with a simple idea: to use the internet to transform book buying into the fastest, easiest, and most enjoyable shopping experience possible.

Over the last decade, we have expanded our offerings to include millions of unique products, from electronics to handmade goods, serving customers in over 25 countries.

Today, we are more than just a marketplace. We are a community of builders, dreamers, and doers, united by our mission to redefine what's possible in global commerce.
